Status Quo

Performance

Affiliate conversion rates in editorial content average at a low

0.5%—1.0%

The average clickthrough rate of display ads is

0.1%

About 1/2 of clicks on mobile ad are accidental

Retailers are looking for new sales distribution solutions to sell in content.

Monitization

Publishers are desperately looking for ways to monetize their content.

Average affiliate commissions are small between

6%—12%

Publishers and retailers are looking for a solution to monetize effectively on mobile.

Data

Publishers can only track limited analytics around their readers purchases and often lose out on commissions.

Retailers have no way to see their performance based on individual posts/articles.

User Experience

Readers have a disrupted experience if they want to purchase an item they are reading about. They are redirected away from the content.

Consumers have several individual checkout carts and have to input their data into each individually.
