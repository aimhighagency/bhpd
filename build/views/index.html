REAL-TIME eCOMMERCE BIDDING PLATFORM

USER PURCHASE

A user discovers a product while browsing their favorite content and triggers the SmartCart. They complete a purchase on the spot and don’t have to think about it again until their purchase arrives directly to their door.

Bidding Pool

Meanwhile, behind the scenes, a bidding pool is activated that allows potential vendors to bid on fulfilling the purchase order based on the demand of the product and negotiated commission rates with Bringhub.

Winning Bid

The vendor with the most favorable bid wins the purchase order automatically, creating a seamless purchase experience for the consumer and distributing commissions in the most efficient way possible based on shifting market conditions.

Product Development & IP

Auto-Content Product Recognition Algorithm

The Bringhub auto-content product recognition algorithm maps generic and/or custom content meta data* to shoppable product groupings (collections). The algorithm works by:

  • Gathering relevant data by means of lexical analysis*
  • Device fingerprinting
  • Image analysis used to curate a list of directly relevant products and products that are peripherally related
  • The algorithm is able to perform these actions in real time across a multitude of platforms

* content meta data - Product SKU, Name, URL, Description, schema.org microdata, explicit product tags

* lexical analysis - Scan page and provide a manifest of all detected "products", also provide device specific details
